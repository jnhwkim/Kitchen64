Kitchen64

Kitchen 64 Dataset for autonomous robots

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

What is this?

This dataset consists of all 512 pictures for 64 locations in an artificial kitchen for experiments. Each location has 8 scenes are collected for various directions, north, north-east, ..., and north-west. Some places are blocked due to obstacles like a refrigerator, dining table, or chairs.

Method to collect

Each location is middle of a square of 30cm x 30cm. The squares are not overlapped in the floor of the kitchen. When the middle point is not available, which means disturbed by an obstacle or cannot located for other reasons, that location is classified as blocked and we did not collect the directional scenes for that location.
